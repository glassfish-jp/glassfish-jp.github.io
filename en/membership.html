Rules and Regulations

Projects

Our activities are based on projects, e.g. planning and holding an evnet. Any members join to each projects.

Boards

There are two types of boards - Directors (JUG Leaders) who lead the group, and Auditor who check and advice to activities and finance.

Board members in 2019:

  • Directors: Norito AGETSUMA, Yoichi KIKUTA and Kenji HASUNUMA
  • Auditor: Takahiro YAMADA

Membership Fee

There is no annual fee. Membership fee is detected by each project as needs. For example, a registration fee of event.

In regard to cash shortage and overage, it is ajusted by whole the group. We have a bank account ourselves for the purpose.

Constitution and other

There is constitution (PDF) to act as a group.
